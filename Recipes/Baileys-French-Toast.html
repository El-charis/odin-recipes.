Baileys French Toast

Description

Baileys French toast is made of fluffy, sweet challah bread soaked in a Baileys-infused egg wash and topped with a decadent chocolate and Baileys sauce. Itâ€™s easy to make, and the Irish cream offers so much flavor that you barely need to add any other ingredients.

Challah or brioche bread works best for this recipe since they are thick, rich, and already have great flavor. However, you can use another bread of your choice. Thinner, less sturdy bread will need less time soaking in the egg wash and will have a shorter cooking time.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup Baileys Irish Cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

STEPS

  • Gather the ingredients.
  • Bailey's French toast ingredients Whisk eggs in a shallow bowl until whites and yolks are completely incorporated.
  • Egg batter whisked together in a ceramic dish
  • Soak each slice of bread in egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides.
  • Whisked eggs in a shallow container Add in milk, sugar, Baileys, and vanilla extract to eggs and whisk again.
  • Soak for a minute or so, longer if bread is extra thick, and less time if you are using a thinner bread.
